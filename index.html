About KillPhilosophy

KillPhilosophy is an academic network exploration tool for understanding connections between philosophers, critical theorists, and other intellectuals.

Features

  • Discover new connections and content through Novelty Feed
  • Search academic profiles with intelligent deep search technology
  • Browse the comprehensive database of academics
  • Contribute to growing the network with vetted, quality information
  • Track intellectual lineages and influences
